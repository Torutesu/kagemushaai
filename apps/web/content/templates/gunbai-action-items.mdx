## Battle Objectives

Define the purpose and desired outcomes of this war council. What must be decided or resolved?

## Intelligence Report

Current status updates from each front. Key metrics, progress reports, and field observations.

## Strategic Decisions

Record all decisions made during the council, including rationale and any dissenting viewpoints.

## Mission Assignments

Action items assigned to each warrior, with clear ownership, deadlines, and expected deliverables.

## Victory Conditions

Measurable criteria that define success. How will we know we have achieved our objectives?
