## Victories

What went well during this campaign? Recognize achievements, successful strategies, and team efforts worth celebrating.

## Fallen Fortresses

What didn't go as planned? Analyze setbacks and extract lessons that will strengthen future campaigns.

## Supply Lines

What is blocking or slowing the team down? Identify dependencies, resource constraints, and bottlenecks that need attention.

## Next Campaign

What will we do differently going forward? Define concrete changes and improvements for the next advance.
