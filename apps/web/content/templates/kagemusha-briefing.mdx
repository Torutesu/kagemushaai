## Situation Overview

Brief summary of the current landscape, market conditions, and organizational state.

## Key Intelligence

Important information gathered during the meeting. Critical data points, insights, and observations.

## Threats & Risks

Potential dangers, competitive threats, and concerns raised. Assess severity and likelihood.

## Opportunities

New possibilities, strategic openings, and favorable conditions identified during the briefing.

## Shadow's Counsel

Recommended actions, prioritized next steps, and strategic guidance based on the intelligence gathered.
